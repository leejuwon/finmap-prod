## 2. What Is DXY?

### ‚óè Definition  
DXY shows the value of the U.S. dollar relative to **six major currencies**.  
The base value is **100 in March 1973**.

### ‚óè Currency weights  
| Currency | Weight (%) |
|----------|------------|
| EUR | 57.6% |
| JPY | 13.6% |
| GBP | 11.9% |
| CAD | 9.1% |
| SEK | 4.2% |
| CHF | 3.6% |

EUR dominates the index, meaning European economic conditions strongly influence DXY.
